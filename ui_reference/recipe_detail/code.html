Harvest Grain Bowl

Calories 420 kcal per serving
Prep Time 15 minutes prep
Carbs 52g
Protein 18g

shopping_basket Ingredients

  • Quinoa, cooked 1 cup
  • Roasted Sweet Potato 1/2 cup
  • Baby Spinach 2 cups
  • Avocado 1/2 large
  • Tahini Dressing 2 tbsp

restaurant Step-by-Step

1

Rinse the quinoa thoroughly and cook according to package instructions until fluffy.

2

Dice the sweet potatoes, toss with olive oil, and roast at 400°F until tender and browned.

3

Whisk together tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, and warm water for a creamy dressing.
